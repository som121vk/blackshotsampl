# Blackshot Design & Customization Guide

## üé® Homepage Customization

Your homepage is now dynamic! You can control the banners and layout directly from the Admin Panel.

### 1. Hero Slider (Top of Page)
The large banner at the very top of the site.
- **Recommended Size**: `1920 x 600 pixels`
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **How to Update**:
  1. Go to **Admin Panel** > **Website Design** tab.
  2. Under "Hero Slider Images", click "Choose File".
  3. Select your wide banner image.
  4. Click "Add Hero Banner".
  5. Repeat to add multiple slides (they will auto-play)!

### 2. Category Circles
These are automatically generated from your Categories.
- **How to Update**:
  1. Go to **Admin Panel** > **Categories** tab.
  2. Add a new category (e.g., "Puzzles").
  3. It will instantly appear as a circle on the homepage!
  4. *Note: Icon colors are auto-generated.*

### 3. Middle Banners (The "Blank Images" Section)
The two side-by-side banners in the middle of the page.
- **Recommended Size**: `600 x 400 pixels`
- **How to Update**:
  1. Go to **Admin Panel** > **Website Design** tab.
  2. Under "Middle Banner Section", click "Choose File".
  3. Select your promo image.
  4. Click "Add Middle Banner".
  5. You should add at least 2 images here for the best look.

## üì¶ Managing Categories

Organize your products properly to help customers find them.

1. **Create a Category**:
   - Admin > Categories > Enter name (e.g., "Drones") > Add.
2. **Assign Product to Category**:
   - Admin > Products > Add/Edit Product.
   - Select "Drones" from the Category dropdown.
3. **Delete Category**:
   - Admin > Categories > Click Trash icon.
   - *Warning: Products in this category will still exist but might need re-categorizing.*
